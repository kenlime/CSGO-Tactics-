Execution

Simply put, this is just an all out B rush. Purple, Blue, Orange and Green (in that order!) run through tunnels onto B. Purple checks left, Blue checks right, Orange throws an HE at back of plat or taint and, then, site should be cleared. Green should get the bomb planted as soon as site is cleared. Yellow watched the rotate through lower tuns.
