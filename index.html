Counter Strike: Global Offensive

Team Based Tactics

Counter Strike: Global Offensive, commonly shortened to CSGO, is a team based, first person shooter (FPS), and is one of the most popular games in the global competitive gaming community. It outweighs the popularity of both Call of Duty and Battlefield. Both of which are very big names in the FPS market.

CSGO has a number of game modes that all can be played as a single player. However, it is best played, with a well oiled team, working together. This web site will focus exclusively on the competitive game mode, explain certain aspects of CSGOâ€™s gameplay mechanics and show examples of how a team can play together.


Basic Mechanics

Team Objectives


The goal of the Terrorist team is to plant a bomb at one of two designated bomb-sites. Discordantly, the goal of the Counter-Terrorist team is to stop the Terrorist team from letting the bomb go off.

Game Layout

Each game consist of two, five man, teams: Terrorist and Counter-Terrorist. Games are best out of 30 rounds (first to 16), with a halftime after round 15. At halftime, teams switch sides. Meaning the Terrorist become the Counter-Terrorist and visa versa.

Each round is two minutes long and can be won in one of three ways: one team kills all five members of the opposing team; the bomb explodes; or the bomb is defused. Most commonly a round is won with one team killing all five members of the opposing team.

Bomb Mechanics

As said earlier, the goal of the Terrorist team is to plant a bomb at one of two designated bomb-sites. Planting the bomb takes four seconds. Then the Terrorist team must defend the bomb until it explodes, 35 seconds later. After the bomb is planted, a member of the Counter-Terrorist team can defuse the bomb. It takes 10 seconds to defuse the bomb without a defuse kit and five seconds with a defuse kit.
